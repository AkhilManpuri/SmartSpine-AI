Health Resources

Ergonomic equipment recommendations for a healthier spine.

🪑

Ergonomic Chairs

Look for chairs with adjustable lumbar support, seat depth, and armrests to maintain the natural curve of your spine.

💻

Laptop Stands

Elevate your screen to eye level to prevent "text neck" and reduce strain on your cervical spine.

🧍

Standing Desks

Alternate between sitting and standing throughout the day to reduce pressure on the lower back.

🦶

Footrests

If your feet don't firmly touch the floor, a footrest helps align your hips and knees to exactly 90 degrees.
